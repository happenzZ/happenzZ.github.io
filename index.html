About Me

Short biography

My name is Haipeng Zeng (曾海鹏). I am currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Intelligent Systems Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). I received the B.S. degree in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China, in 2014 and the Ph.D. degree from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) co-supervised by Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Ting-Chuen PONG, Hong Kong, in 2020.

Research Interest

  • Data visualization, intelligent visual analysis of multimedia data (video, image, text, sound, etc.)
  • Intelligent transportation, visual analysis of urban data
  • Machine learning, explainable artificial intelligence

Projects

Personalized Real-time Air-quality Informatics System for Exposure - Hong Kong (PRAISE-HK)

  • November 2016 - August 2018; A five-year project.
  • Implemented a web-based prototype of the Urban AQ modeling (PRAISE-AQ).
  • Further improved the PRAISE-AQ prototype for AQ prediction with real data, and characterization the PRAISE-AQ prototype for different indoor/outdoor microenvironment.

Visualization of Heterogeneous Data Integration in Thales Project

  • January 2016 - December 2017; A ITF theme-based project in Hong Kong.
  • Put different types of spatio-temporal data into a unified visualization platform to facilitate data fusion and integration.
  • Provided visual analytics techniques for video tracking data and social media data, and visualization methods for displaying the motions of thousands of individuals.
  • Implemented visualization modules for the smart-transportation system and equipment early warning system, which facilitate crowd's mobility and safety analysis.
