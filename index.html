Haipeng Zeng (曾海鹏)

I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Intelligent Systems Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). I received my Ph.D. at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), where my supervisors are Prof. Huamin Qu and Prof. Ting-Chuen PONG. Prior to HKUST, I received my B.S. degree in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), China.

Research Interests:

  • Data Visualization & Visual Analytics
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Intelligent Transportation
Multimodal Human Communication | Explainable AI | Urban Computing/Smart City
